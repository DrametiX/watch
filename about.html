About DrametiX

Where Asian stories come to life

Our Story

DrametiX was born from a deep passion for Asian storytelling. Founded in 2025 in Ranchi, Jharkhand, we set out to create a beautiful, user-friendly platform where fans can easily discover and enjoy the best Korean dramas, Chinese dramas, Japanese series, Thai BL, and Asian films.

What started as a small project to share hidden gems has grown into a curated destination trusted by thousands of drama lovers across India and beyond.

Our Mission

To bring the richest and most emotional Asian stories directly to your screen — with love, respect, and zero clutter. We believe every great drama deserves to be found, felt, and remembered.

What We Offer

  • Hand-curated collections of trending and timeless K-Dramas, C-Dramas, and Asian movies
  • Regular updates with the latest releases and upcoming premieres (2026 & beyond)
  • Clean, cinematic interface designed for drama lovers
  • Simple discovery tools — no login required, no ads interrupting your experience
  • Community-driven recommendations and honest vibes
